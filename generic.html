A Generic Page

In this project, I and my friend Ben create a system for grade-tracking since we know most of us only bother to calculate our grades around the time when finals are coming up since it is not convenient to calculate total grades manually. One of our main goals for the project is to create a Python application that enables students to efficiently track their grades. This means putting in place functionalities for adding new courses, accurately recording grades, computing overall course grades, and producing thorough grade summaries. Additionally, a well-designed user interface is essential for our project to guarantee accessibility and ease of use.
